"Blue Springs! One Team!"

Welcome to our vibrant speech and debate team! We're all about creating an open and inclusive environment where every member can thrive. From supporting novice members through our membership program to organizing engaging social events and team-building activites, we're dedicated to fostering a strong sense of community. Our team is known for its passion, dedication, and drive to excel in competitions and tournaments. We also believe in the power of collaboration, encouraging our members to work on exciting and innovative projects together. We're commited to honing our skills in public speaking and debate. Join us on this incredible journey as we aim to make a lasting impact both within our team and beyond!
