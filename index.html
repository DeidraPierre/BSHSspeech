Blue Spring High Speech and Debate


What is Speech and Debate?

A competitive activity that focuses on developing students' skills in communication, critical thinking, and argumentation. Participants engage in various events, including public speaking, debate, and interpretation, where they present and defend ideas on a range of topics. Through rigorous preparation and performance, students learn to craft compelling arguments, think on their feet, and communicate effectively with diverse audiences

Why should I join?

Joining our Speech and Debate team is a fantastic opportunity to enhance your voice and your future. You'll gain skills in persuasion, logic, and quick thinking, all while challenging yourself in a supportive environment. As a member, you'll learn to express your ideas clearly, think critically under pressure, and collaborate with like-minded peers. Whether competing in debates or showcasing your talent in speeches and performances, you'll build lasting friendships, improve your leadership skills, and prepare for college and career success. It's an exciting chance to grow, compete, and make a difference!
